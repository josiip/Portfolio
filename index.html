About Me.

Product designer creating graphics for print and digital media, crafting interfaces that are simple, clear, and easy to use. I’m experienced in working with clients across different industries and value the trust and clear communication that come from these collaborations.

My work process focuses on simple solutions, adapts to project needs and stays clear from start to finish. Decisions are made from the client’s perspective, with attention to how the final result will be used and understood in real-world situations.

Skilled in Adobe Creative Suite, Figma and always exploring new ways to improve and keep my work fresh.

Selected Works.

DropData

Product Design · SaaS Dashboard

A data-driven SaaS dashboard designed to monitor billboard performance using camera input and traffic data. The system provides real-time insights such as vehicle counts, movement patterns, and ad exposure, helping advertisers better understand reach and optimize campaign performance.

Street Food Fest

Visual Identity · Digital & Print

Complete visual identity for a local street food festival, covering the logo, core visuals, social media, and print materials. The result is a bold, flexible visual system that works naturally across both digital and on-site use.

GoTeam

Product Design · Mobile Application

A mobile app designed to help groups plan trips together by collecting preferences, aligning availability, and guiding the group toward a shared decision. The focus was on clear flows, simple input, and smooth decision-making across the entire booking process.

globalsoft

Website Redesign · UX/UI & Digital Assets

Website redesign focused on simplifying the structure and rebuilding the landing page around selected work. Projects are presented through a rotating layout that highlights key case studies and guides users deeper into the site, while unnecessary content was removed to keep the experience clear and focused.

Frutinator

Casino Game Interface · Visual Design

Visual interface design for a classic slot game, focused on clear gameplay structure, recognizable symbols, and strong visual feedback during key moments like wins. The experience was designed to feel familiar and easy to follow, with emphasis on symbol clarity, contrast, and win-state presentation to keep gameplay readable and engaging.
